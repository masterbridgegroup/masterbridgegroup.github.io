Our Story

A bridge from Seoul to Southeast Asia

After twenty years living across Vietnam and Thailand, our founder discovered something the world had overlooked — the quiet mastery of Korea's craftspeople in the workshops of Dongdaemun.

KEDIT SEOUL was born to carry that craftsmanship across borders: a curated K-lifestyle brand for women who notice the difference between what is made, and what is made well.
